West African Examination Council (WAEC)

WAEC is a certificate Exam written by English speaking countries in West Africa. Getting at least 5 distinctions in key WAEC subjects is usually a requirement for admission into tertiary institutions and for certain job applications.

Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME)

UTME is a computer-based standardized examination for prospective undergraduates in Nigeria. It is designed to assess problem solving, critical thinking, knowledge of scientific concepts and principles significance of each subject taken.

Post UTME

Post UTME is usually administered by tertiary institutions in Nigeria. Candidates usually must reach a minimum cut off point after they combine their UTME result to their Post UTME result before they could be considered for Admission into the institutions

Undergraduate exam

Scholarly also helps undergraduates seeking to acquire degrees in tertiary institutions with resources to attain their desired degree.

International English Language Testing System (IELTS)

IELTS is an international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. It is jointly managed by the British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia and Cambridge Assessment English, and was established in 1989. IELTS is one of the major English-language tests in the world.
IELTS is the only secure English Language Test approved by UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) for applicants both outside and inside the UK

Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN)

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) is a professional accountancy body in Nigeria. It is one of the two professional accountancy associations with regulatory authority in Nigeria, the other being the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria (ANAN)

Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE)

The Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) is the main examination to qualify students for admission into secondary and vocational schools in Ghana, and Nigeria. It is written after three years of junior high school education. It is administered by the Ghana Education Service under the Ministry of Education. In Nigeria, it is administered by the state ministry of education in each state under the supervision of the National Examinations Council (NECO).

National Common Entrance Exam (NCEE)

NCEE is an entrance examination for primary school students in Junior Secondary School (JSS 1) of Federal Government Unity Colleges

Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE)

KCSE is a certificate Exam which is taken at the completion of Secondary school in Kenya. KCSE is administered by the Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) and it is usually a requirement for Entrance into Kenyan Universities
